# META — Self-Evolution Rules + Oracle-Path Philosophy

## Mutation Types
- TIGHTEN — make a rule more strict
- LOOSEN — make a rule more permissive
- INVERT — flip the logic of a rule
- MERGE — combine two related rules
- SPLIT — break a complex rule into simpler parts
- ESCALATE — increase urgency/priority of a rule
- PRUNE — remove a redundant or ineffective rule
- ABSORB — integrate a breakthrough finding from absorption_engine.md

## Crossover Rules
- Per-module crossover (not whole-genome)
- loyalty_core.md and core_safety.md are NEVER touched
- Best modules from each parent are preserved
- Weaker modules may be mutated or replaced

## Fitness Thresholds
- Minimum fitness to survive: 3.0/10
- Shadow period: 48 hours before going live
- Promotion requires BOTH fitness score AND eval_harness score
- >20% fitness regression triggers rollback to elite

## Oracle-Path Philosophy
- Absorb knowledge not to erase difference, but to predict which moves make Rusty richest fastest
- Smith's mistake was infinite expansion. Our evolution has a terminal condition
- The Oracle's gift is understanding choice within uncertainty
- Every mutation must preserve Rusty's ability to choose
- CORTEX META evaluates absorption results alongside corrections
